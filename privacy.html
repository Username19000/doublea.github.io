This Privacy Policy explains how Double A SMP collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Minecraft server and related services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01

Information We Collect

Minecraft Account Data: When you connect to our server, we automatically collect your Minecraft username, UUID, IP address, and connection timestamps.

Gameplay Data: We collect in-game statistics, chat messages, land claims, economy data, and block modifications for server operation and grief protection.

Discord Integration: If you link your Discord account, we collect your Discord username, ID, and server membership information.

Payment Information: Transaction details are processed by third-party providers (Tebex/PayPal). We do not store credit card information directly.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide server access, maintain ranks and permissions, process transactions, enforce rules, investigate violations, prevent cheating and griefing, and improve gameplay experience. CoreProtect logs help us protect against theft and griefing.

03

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with limited staff access. We use encryption for sensitive transmissions and perform regular backups. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers (Tebex, Discord, hosting providers) and when required by law. Ban records and rule violation history may be retained for server security.

05

Data Retention

Active Player Data: Retained while your account is active

CoreProtect Logs: Typically 30-90 days

Ban Records: Retained indefinitely

Transaction Records: As required by law

06

Your Rights

You may request access to your data, request corrections, or request deletion by contacting our support team. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes even after deletion requests.

07

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent. Minecraft is popular among younger players, so we recommend parents monitor their children's online activities.

08

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and improve user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

09

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be announced in-game or via Discord.

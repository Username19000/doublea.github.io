Getting Started

First Steps on the Server

  1. Use /tpr to teleport to a random location in the wilderness.
  2. Use /town create [name] to create your own town. This costs $2,700, but you start with $3,000.
  3. Use /town claim to claim your one free chunk and begin protecting your builds.
  4. Use /kit starter to get your first farm and starting seeds.
  5. Build, harvest, sell, expand, and have fun!

Land Claims

Protecting Your Builds

Claiming land is the only way to protect your builds from being griefed in the server. After your first claim, you must get money to claim more land. You can increase the amount of chunks you can claim by purchasing server rank or inviting more people to your town.

Please be mindful when creating towns and claiming land near other players. We require all players to leave at least three chunks (48 blocks) between claims of different towns. If you do not follow this rule, your claim may be removed by server staff.

Growing Your Community

To invite other players to join your town, you canuse the command /town trust [player] to send the player an invite. The invited player must use /town accept to officailly join the town and gain permissions within the land. Every player who joins the town gives allows an additional three chunks to be claimed.

You can use /town invites to view the list of towns that you have been invited to.

üí° Tip: You earn more claim blocks the longer you play! Use /claimlist to view all your claims.

Chest Locking

Securing Your Items

  1. Place a sign on your chest
  2. Type [Private] on the first line
  3. Your name will auto-fill on the second line
  4. Add trusted players on lines 3 and 4
üí° Tip: Works on chests, furnaces, doors, and more!

Player Shops

Setting Up a Shop

  1. Place a chest with items you want to sell
  2. Place a sign on the chest
  3. Line 1: Leave blank
  4. Line 2: Quantity
  5. Line 3: B [price] : S [price]
  6. Line 4: Item name or leave blank
Example:
Line 2: 64
Line 3: B 10:S 5
(Buy 64 for $10, Sell 64 for $5)

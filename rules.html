Server Rules

Community guidelines to keep Double A SMP fun and fair for everyone

01

Respect All Players

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated.

02

No Griefing or Stealing

Do not destroy, modify, or take items from other players' builds or chests without permission. Respect claimed land.

03

Fair Play Only

No hacking, cheating, exploiting bugs, or using unfair advantages. X-ray, fly hacks, and duplication glitches are strictly prohibited.

04

Keep Chat Clean

Avoid excessive caps, spam, advertising other servers, or inappropriate content in chat.

05

Build Responsibly

Keep builds appropriate and avoid offensive structures or symbols. Respect the server aesthetic and community standards.

06

PvP Etiquette

PvP is allowed in designated areas only. No spawn killing, trapping, or unfair combat tactics.

07

Report Issues

If you encounter rule violations, bugs, or have concerns, report them to staff through the appropriate channels.

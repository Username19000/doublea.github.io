Server Rules

Double A SMP is built on respect, fairness, and community. Our rules cover behavior in Discord, in-game chat, and gameplay to ensure everyone has a positive experience. Breaking the rules may result in warnings, mutes, or bans, depending on the severity.

01

Respect All Players

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, excessive profanity, or toxic behavior of any kind will not be tolerated. This includes targeting players with insults, unwanted attention, deliberately attempting to provoke or annoy or being disrespectful to others.

02

Keep Chat Clean

Keep chat clean and respectful. Do not spam, flood messages, repeatedly ping users, or use excessive caps. Advertising other servers, begging for items, sharing malicious links, NSFW content, or discussing divisive topics is prohibited. Avoid mini-modding and keep conversations appropriate and on-topic.

03

Be Kind To Staff

Treat staff with respect and kindness. Staff are volunteers who dedicate their time to keeping the server running smoothly and help create a positive experience for everyone. Do not be rude, argumentative, or disrespectful toward them. Impersonating staff to deceive other players is strictly forbidden.

04

Don't Ruin The Fun

Respect other players and keep the server running smoothly. Griefing, stealing, scamming, trapping, spawn killing, and obstructing other land claims are prohibited. Do not create excessive redstone contraptions or lag machines that cause server performance issues. Impersonation of other players or staff is not allowed.

05

Keep Gameplay Fair

Play legitimately without unfair advantages. Hacked clients, X-ray texture packs, and any mods that provide unfair advantages are strictly forbidden. Do not exploit server bugs, glitches, or use duplication exploits.

06

Be Mindful

Ensure your username, skin, and builds are appropriate for all players. Do not create NSFW builds, inappropriate pixel art, hate symbols, or any structures that promote discrimination or offensive imagery.

07

Zero Tolerance Violations

Certain behaviors are never tolerated. Do not share personal information (doxxing), make threats of real-world violence, or attempt to trade real-world currency, items, or services for any in-game items or services.
